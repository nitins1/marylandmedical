Steps to obtain medical marijuana in maryland:

Step 1

Signup for the Patient Registry

The Patient Registry is operated by the Maryland Medical Cannabis Commission (MMCC) and opens sometime in the spring of 2017—We'll let you know when it does. After signing up you’ll receive your patient number. Obtaining a Patient ID card is optional.

Step 2

Find your physician

Every physician on our website is registered with the MMCC. You give your physician your patient number, which they enter into a secure database in order to issue an online certification for medical marijuana.

Step 3

Find your dispensary

After finding an accessible dispensary, simply present your government-issued ID card (e.g., driver’s license or passport) for proof of identity, and the dispensary clerk will check to see that your physician entered you on the secure database.
