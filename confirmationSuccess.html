Steps to obtain medical marijuana in Maryland:

Step 1

Signup for the Patient Registry

The Patient Registry is operated by the Maryland Medical Cannabis Commission (MMCC) and is currently open. After signing up you’ll receive your patient number. Obtaining a Patient ID card is optional.

Step 2

Find your physician

We show physicians available all over the state. You give your physician your patient number, and they'll enter it into a secure database and issue you an online certification for medical marijuana.

Step 3

Find your dispensary

After finding an accessible dispensary, simply present your government-issued ID card (e.g., driver’s license or passport) for proof of identity, and the dispensary clerk will check to see that you're on the secure database and were issued the certification by your physician.
